Biography

Handong Cui obtained his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science (CS) at The University of Hong Kong (香港大學) under the supervision of Prof. John T. H. Yuen and Prof. S. M. Yiu. He obtained his Bachelor of Engineering Degree from Sichuan University (四川大學)。 His research interests focus on cryptography, security and privacy issue in blockchain.
