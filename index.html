Biography

Handong Cui is currently a Fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong, co-advised by Dr. John T. H. Yuen and Prof. S. M. Yiu. His research mainly focuses on cryptography, blockchain and privacy-enhancing technologies (threshold signature, zero-knowledge proof). He received his B.Eng. degree in information security from Sichuan University (SCU) in 2019. He used to work as a risk management intern in APG Asset Management Asia.
