About

Handong Cui obtained his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science (CS) at The University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. John T. H. Yuen and Prof. S. M. Yiu. He obtained his Bachelor of Engineering Degree from Sichuan University.

His research interests focus on cryptography, security and privacy issues in blockchain. Specifically, he works on zero-knowledge proofs, threshold ECDSA, secure multi-party computation and Web3 technologies.
