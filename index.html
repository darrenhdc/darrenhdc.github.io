Biography

Handong Cui obtained his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science (CS) at The University of Hong Kong (HKU)
in June 2024, under the supervision of Prof. John T. H. Yuen and Prof. S. M. Yiu. His research interests focus on cryptography, security and privacy.
