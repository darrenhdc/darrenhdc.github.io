Biography

Handong Cui obtained his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science (CS) at The University of Hong Kong (HKU) under the supervision of Prof. John T. H. Yuen and Prof. S. M. Yiu. His research interests focus on cryptography.

  • Zero-knowledge proof
  • Threshold cryptography
  • Secure multi-party computation
  • Exotic digital signatures
  • Security and privacy in blockchain
