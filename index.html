Biography

Handong Cui obtained his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at The University of Hong Kong in December 2024, under the supervision of Prof. John T. H. Yuen and Prof. S. M. Yiu. His Ph.D. thesis is Trustless Digital Signatures in Blockchain. He obatined a B.E. degree in information security from Sichuan University in June 2019, where he is supervised by Prof. Cheng Huang.
